Who we are and what we do:

Welcome to the Homepage for Memphis Dubs! We are a local Memphis Esports Team that travels the south in order to participate in various types of Esports tournaments/events. We currently are active in three communities: the Fighting Game Community, Shooter Community, and MOBA Community. If fortune favors us, we are also looking to increase our range of travel to country-wide and potentially even globally.
